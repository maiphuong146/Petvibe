FAQ
What kind of photo do I need to submit? +
Use a clear, well-lit front-facing photo of your pet. Avoid blurry or cropped images — we need to see the full face and ears to create the best embroidery possible.
Can I preview the design before it's embroidered? +
Absolutely! After you place your order, our artist will digitize your pet's portrait and send you a free mock-up for approval via email before we start embroidering.
How long does delivery take? +
We typically need 2–3 days to digitize and embroider your design, plus 3–5 days for shipping depending on your location. Total: around 5–7 days to your door.
What if I'm not happy with the mock-up? +
No worries — we offer unlimited free revisions until you're 100% satisfied. If the design doesn't meet your expectations, we'll issue a full refund.
Is the embroidery durable and long-lasting? +
Yes! We use premium embroidery thread and industrial-grade machines to ensure your design stays vivid and intact through countless washes without fading or peeling.
